Tutoring

City Tech provides students with a variety of tools they can utilize for tutoring and one of the best reliable tools is Tutor.com.
Tutor.com is a free online tutoring service that has a variety of subjects and is always active 24/7. To access it you can log into
you're Blackboard and in the Organizations tab on the right side of the screen click on "click here if you need tutoring", once you
do that you will be brought to a new tab where you will see tutor.com on the left side. Once on the site create an account and start
you're learning.

Job board websites

Bellow are some job boards you may look at to help your path in searching for the job or career you want to pursue.

  • LinkedIn is job search site that has more of a social media type of platform and people often use it to search and post jobs on
    there. LinkedIn
  • Diversify tech is a great website you can use to search for a job in the technology field, and provides a great interactive feel. Diversify Tech
  • Indeed is one of the most common and popular job boards out there right now and provides a simple and easy way to apply for jobs. Indeed
  • Handshake is a job board that is affiliated with CUNY and works with CUNY students across many colleges. As a CityTech student
    you have free access to use handshake and start your job search journey through there. Handshake
