Welcome to the CityTech internship page

Hello and welcome to the CityTech internship assistance page.
CityTech offers assistance for students who are in need of searching for internships or jobs, there are many fields that students are interested in and our job is to assist the students on getting the help they need in finding something that is what they would like to pursue. All the help you can need is right here and we will gladly assist you, below are links to different job boards that is a good start to look for what you want to do.

Tech internships

Tech internships are one of the most sought after fields in the world and the CST department offers many resources in that field of work.

The objective for the internship is to provide the students with:

  • Interact with professionals in the field.
  • Gain experience in the specific field that is chosen.
  • Develop professional relationships.

CityTech computer systems department has many different sites that can be used as suggestions like Goldman Sachs, MTA and CUNY.
