                        Welcome to the CUNY 2X page!

Our goal is to increase the amount of CST students that graduate and have them placed into a full time role in the tech industry by graduation.

  • More info

Goals

  • Double the number of Computer Systems Technology graduates
  • Double the number of graduates who were employed within 6 months of graduation



The Vision

Our vision is to help City Tech build a strong industry informed support system for students. The primary return of investment for students in college is the promise of landing a job with the time, effort, and money they have spent in college. We want them to be equipped with the necessary skills where they can be confident in presenting themselves as professionals

So what's this all about?

With our main goal of doubling the number of CST grads in the coming semesters in order to make sure students have the skills they need we are in the process of having a City Tech Cohort of TTPR(The Tech Talent Pipeline Residency)

This is a year long program for CST students. There is an application process and assessments. Once accepted into the program, they will go through a project based bootcamp for technical training, professional development training with whole group and 1 on 1 coaching sessions, and they will be placed into internships that is subsidized by Mayorâ€™s office.
